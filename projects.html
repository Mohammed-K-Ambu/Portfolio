Projects

CAP Migration

In my internship, I participated in "CAP Migration" a GIS data migration project, where I developed scripts that facilitated the transfer of critical data from an outdated system to the Ericsson Network Engineer (ENE). Working with a small team, we achieved the migration in 40% of the anticipated time and saved the company over $100,000.

GIS Task

I led the design and management of "GIS Task," an innovative application designed to streamline the connection of fiber customers directly from the site. This pioneering project, the first of its kind in the Middle East, optimized the use of human resources by reallocating staff to other critical tasks and enhanced the precision in tracking company assets. The successful deployment of this application marked a significant advancement in telecommunications workflow management.
